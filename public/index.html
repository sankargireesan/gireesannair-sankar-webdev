Hungry Huskies

Hungry Huskies is a MEAN stack application that suggests you some of the best food joints in a particular location. In addition to this, it acts as a platform for foodies, where they can like, review and give ratings to restaurants, follow other users and critics and know about their favorite food destinations. The website uses data from Yelp to display list of restaurants based on search results and also to display details about a restaurant like exact location, rating, etc. In addition to normal users, there are two different roles - critics and owners. Owners invite critics to their restaurant. Critics write authentic reviews about restaurants after visiting the restaurant.
